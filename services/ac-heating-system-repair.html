AC & Heating System Repair

Keep your cabin comfortable with expert AC and heater service.

Schedule this service

Overview

A malfunctioning AC or heater can make driving miserable. We diagnose and repair all climate control systems, from refrigerant leaks to blower motor failures. Our technicians are trained to handle both standard and dual-zone systems.

What's included

  • Restored comfort in all seasons
  • Elimination of strange odors or noises
  • Proper defrosting for safety

How it works

1

System Check

We test pressures, temperatures, and inspect components for leaks or damage.

2

Diagnose

Using electronic leak detectors and thermal imaging, we pinpoint the issue.

3

Repair & Recharge

We replace faulty parts, evacuate the system, and recharge with the correct refrigerant.

Common questions

Why is my AC blowing warm air?

Common causes include low refrigerant, a faulty compressor, or an electrical issue. We can diagnose and fix it.

How often should I service my car's AC?

We recommend an AC check every two years or if you notice reduced performance.
