Overview

Our technicians use cutting-edge diagnostic equipment to pinpoint engine problems quickly. Whether it's a check engine light, rough idling, or loss of power, we provide thorough repairs that restore performance and reliability.

What's included

  • Accurate diagnosis reduces guesswork and saves money
  • Restored engine performance and fuel efficiency
  • Prevents minor issues from becoming major repairs

How it works

1

Comprehensive Scan

We connect your vehicle to our diagnostic system to read error codes and analyze live data.

2

3

Common questions

How long does an engine diagnostic take?

Most diagnostics are completed within an hour. If further inspection is needed, we'll keep you informed.

Will my check engine light come back after repair?

We address the underlying issue, so the light should stay off. If it returns, we'll re-diagnose at no extra charge within our warranty period.
