Overview

Your safety depends on reliable brakes. We inspect, repair, and replace brake pads, rotors, calipers, and fluid to ensure optimal stopping power. Our team uses quality parts and follows manufacturer specifications.

What's included

  • Enhanced safety and shorter stopping distances
  • Quieter, smoother braking performance
  • Extended brake system life with proper maintenance

How it works

1

Thorough Inspection

We check brake pads, rotors, calipers, lines, and fluid levels.

2

Necessary Repairs

We replace worn components with high-quality parts and machine rotors if needed.

3

Final Testing

After repair, we test the brakes on the road to confirm proper function and pedal feel.

Common questions

How often should I replace brake pads?

Typically every 30,000 to 70,000 miles depending on driving habits. We recommend an inspection at every oil change.

What are signs of brake trouble?

Squealing, grinding, a soft pedal, or vibration when braking. If you notice any, bring your car in immediately.
